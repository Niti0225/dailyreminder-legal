Why don’t notifications show up?

Check iOS Settings → Notifications → DailyReminder and make sure notifications are enabled. Also check Focus modes, Low Power Mode, and Notification Summary.

How do I delete all data?

In the app: Settings → Advanced → “Delete all data”.

How can I contact support?

Email Dailyreminder@hotmail.com and include the “Diagnostics” text from the app.
