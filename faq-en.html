Why do notifications sometimes not arrive?

iOS may limit notifications through Focus, battery saving, permissions, or system settings.

Where are my reminders stored?

The app stores reminders locally on your device unless you actively export or share them.

What does Premium unlock?

Premium may unlock more active reminders, backups, longer stats history, challenges, and an ad-free experience.
