Privacy Policy

For the “DailyReminder” iOS app. Last updated:

1. Controller

Controller (GDPR):
DailyReminder (private project)
Email: Dailyreminder@hotmail.com

2. Data processed by the app

DailyReminder stores reminders you create (title, schedules/intervals, completion status/statistics) locally on your device. The app uses device storage (UserDefaults).

3. Notifications

If you enable notifications, the app uses Apple’s notification system to display reminders on your device. The app does not send your reminder content to us as the developer.

4. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The free version may display ads using Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads SDK. This may involve processing device information (e.g. Advertising Identifier, IP address, approximate location) to deliver and measure ads.

More information: Google Privacy Policy.

5. Subscriptions / purchases (future)

If subscriptions are offered in the future, payments will be processed via Apple In-App Purchases. A service like RevenueCat may be used to manage subscription status. This policy will be updated accordingly.

6. Your rights

Under GDPR you have rights such as access, rectification, deletion, restriction, and objection. Since the app stores data primarily locally, you can usually delete data directly in the app (e.g. “Delete all data” in Settings).
